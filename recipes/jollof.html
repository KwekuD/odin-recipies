How To Prepare Jollof(My Secret recipe)

Origin of Jollof

Jollof is common amongst countries in West Africa. Its a rice based meal and can be taken alone. Its a very popular food

It can be taken with chicken, fish and boiled or fried egg

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • pepper
  • sunflower oil
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • Maggi cubes
  • curry powder
  • rosemary spice
  • lemon juice
  • cucumber, carrot
  • fried chunks of goat meat

Procedure

  1. Make tomatoe sauce and keep overnight
  2. add the tomatoe sauce in a new pot over fire and add some lemon juice about half a glass
  3. Wash rice
  4. add the rice to the stew and add some water
  5. lower the gas to very low heat and allow the rice to cook
  6. Stir the rice intermittently and carefully
  7. add rosemary
  8. chop carrot and cucumber into small pieces and add to the rice
  9. when rice is almost soft, cover with a clean napkin
  10. add the fried goat meat and allow to cook for a while
  11. Food is ready after 5 mins.....serve hot
