Banku Recipe

Origin of Bnaku

Banku is a Ghanaian delicacy common amongst the people of Asante, Fante and Ga communities. It is prepared from corn dough, mixed with half portions of cassava dough

It can be taken with okroh soup, ground pepper, shito, groundnut soup, palmnut soup.

Ingredients

  • Corn dough
  • cassava dough
  • maggi cube
  • water
  • salt

Procedure

  1. Mix the corn dough with two portions of water into a pot
  2. Add half a portion of cassava dough and mix till paste is even
  3. Add some salt and maggi cube to improve taste
  4. Stir and drive on fire till it becomes solid
  5. Serve with any soup or ground pepper and grilled Tilapia
